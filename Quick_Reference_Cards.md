## MEMORY TRICKS & MNEMONICS

### 15 DINO NAMES: "TTTVBAASPDAP"
- **T** = **T**riceratops, **T**yrannosaurus rex
- **V** = **V**elociraptor
- **B** = **B**rachiosaurus
- **A** = **A**patosaurus, **A**llosaurus, **A**nkylosaurus, **A**crocanthosaurus
- **S** = **S**tegosaurus, **S**pinosaurus
- **P** = **P**arasaurolophus, **P**lateosaurus
- **D** = **D**iplodocus
- **P** = Plus... (Other species too!)

### 5 FOSSIL TYPES: "BMCTC"
- **B**ody fossil
- **M**old
- **C**ast
- **T**race fossil
- **C**arbonized film

### GEOLOGIC ERAS: "TJC" or "TV Dinner"
- **T** = **T**riassic (oldest)
- **J** = **J**urassic (middle)
- **C** = **C**retaceous (newest)

Or "**TV Dinner**" for each era's character:
- **T** = Beginning (**T**riassic first dinos)
- **V** = Variety & **V**iolence (**J**urassic - lots of dinos fighting)
- **D** = Diverse & **D**estruction (**C**retaceous - then extinction)

### TRIASSIC DINOS: "CP"
- **C**oelophysis
- **P**lateosaurus

### JURASSIC DINOS: "DASAB"
- **D**iplodocus
- **A**patosaurus
- **S**tegosaurus
- **A**llosaurus
- **B**rachiosaurus (+ Archaeopteryx)

### CRETACEOUS DINOS: "TTVAPSAA"
- **T**yrannosaurus, **T**riceratops
- **V**elociraptor
- **A**nkylosaurus, **A**crocanthosaurus
- **P**arasaurolophus
- **S**pinosaurus
